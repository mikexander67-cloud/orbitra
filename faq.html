FAQ

Questions, answered plainly.

How payments work, what's in scope, how online stores take payment, and how the Care Plan works. If something isn't here, the audit is the best place to ask.

Payments
How do I pay Orbitra?

Payments to Orbitra are made by MCB Juice — send-to-mobile to Orbitra's business Juice number — or by direct bank transfer to Orbitra's registered Mauritian business account. Payment instructions are included in the scope document at signing.

Orbitra does not take card payments. Stripe, MIPS, and PayPal are never used for paying Orbitra.

How do the installments work?

Every package is split into a deposit plus one or more installments. Service packages are typically a deposit plus the balance at handover, or a deposit plus two installments on the larger packages. Ecommerce packages are a 40% deposit plus the balance over two installments, matched to delivery milestones. The exact split for your package is shown on the package and confirmed in your scope document.

Is my deposit refundable?

The deposit is refundable within 7 days of signing, as long as work has not yet begun. Once discovery work starts, the deposit becomes non-refundable. Installments paid against completed milestones are non-refundable.

What happens if I miss an installment?

Work pauses until the installment is settled, and the timeline shifts by the length of the pause. There are no late penalties — just talk to us. The schedule is there to keep things clear for both sides, not to catch anyone out.

How your store takes payment
How will my online store accept payments from customers?

For ecommerce packages, the Shopify store we build is configured at checkout with Stripe, MIPS, and MCB Juice send-to-mobile — so your end-customers can pay the way that suits them.

This is separate from how you pay Orbitra. Stripe and MIPS only ever appear in the context of your store's checkout, for your customers — never as a way to pay Orbitra.

Are there ongoing costs with an ecommerce package?

Yes — and they're disclosed openly on every ecommerce package. The Shopify subscription is Rs 1,600/month, paid directly to Shopify. The Orbitra Care Plan is optional and recommended, from Rs 2,667/month. If you take the Care Plan, total ongoing cost is roughly Rs 4,267 to Rs 5,100/month depending on which commitment tier you choose.

Scope
Why packages instead of a custom quote?

A defined package means you can see exactly what you're getting, what it costs, and how long it takes — before you commit. It keeps pricing fixed and fair, and it keeps the process the same for every business. Custom quotes tend to be slower, less predictable, and harder to budget for.

What's included, and what isn't?

Each package lists its deliverables in full on the Packages page — website, branding, social, training, and support. What's not included: custom one-off creative direction outside the package, unlimited revisions, and services not defined in the package list. If you need something extra, many additions are available as add-ons or upsell packages at published prices.

Can I change the scope after signing?

Small adjustments within the package are part of the normal process. Anything beyond the package — extra pages, a booking system, an ecommerce module — is handled as a clearly priced add-on or upsell, agreed before the work is done. Nothing is added to your bill without your say-so.

Care Plan
What does the Care Plan cover?

The Care Plan keeps your hosting, domain, and business email active, runs daily backups and security monitoring, includes up to two minor content updates a month, and sends a monthly performance snapshot. Additional updates beyond the two are Rs 1,500 each, billed transparently.

What are the commitment tiers?

Four tiers, with longer commitments unlocking savings. Monthly is Rs 3,500/month with no commitment. Quarterly is Rs 9,500/quarter (about Rs 3,167/month). Semi-annual is Rs 17,500 for six months (about Rs 2,917/month). Annual is Rs 32,000/year (about Rs 2,667/month). You can upgrade to a longer commitment at any time, with the difference prorated.

Is there a discount for taking it at launch?

Yes. If you take the annual Care Plan at the same time as a launch package, it's Rs 28,000 for the first year instead of Rs 32,000 — a one-time Rs 4,000 saving at point of launch. After the first year it renews at standard tier pricing.

What if I don't want the Care Plan?

That's a real option, and it's fine. Every package hands over full admin access to your dashboard, your hosting and domain credentials in your own name, a step-by-step manual, and recordings of the training calls — everything you need to self-manage.

Without an active Care Plan, Orbitra won't make updates on your behalf, monitor your site, handle hosting or domain renewals, or restore the site if something breaks. If you need occasional help, one-off support is available at Rs 1,500/hour with a 30-minute minimum. You can also subscribe to the Care Plan later, from any tier, with no penalty for having declined it earlier.
