01 / Pricing & Payment

How pricing works, and how you pay.

Every package has a fixed price and a clear payment schedule. No hidden fees, no surprise invoices at handover.

Fixed. Every Orbitra package has a published price on the Packages page. What you see is what you pay.

If your needs fall outside a package, we’ll recommend the closest fit plus any add-ons. The total is confirmed in your scope document before you commit.

Deposits start the work. Balances are tied to delivery milestones, not arbitrary dates.

  • Starter Launch — 40% deposit, 60% at handover
  • Full & Premium Launch — 40% deposit, then two equal installments
  • Ecommerce packages — 40% deposit, balance over two installments

Installments are scheduled in your signed scope document so you always know what’s next.

Local bank transfer in MUR is preferred. We’ll send a proper invoice for each installment with our account details.

Cash and other arrangements can be discussed during the scope review if needed.

No. Packages are priced so we can deliver the full scope without cutting corners. Holding prices steady is how we keep them fair for everyone.

If a package feels out of reach, the Starter Launch is built for early-stage businesses on a tight budget.

02 / Scope & Timelines

What’s in, what’s out, and how long it takes.

Every deliverable is listed in your scope document. Timelines assume you supply content and feedback on time.

  • Starter Launch — 30 days
  • Full Launch — 60 days
  • Premium Launch — 75 days
  • Ecommerce Starter — 45 days · Full — 60 days · Premium — 90 days

The clock starts when the deposit clears and we have your kickoff content. Delays in feedback or assets shift the launch date.

Two structured revision rounds per milestone — branding, design, and content. We collect all feedback together rather than reacting piece by piece.

Major scope changes mid-build are quoted as add-ons so the project stays on schedule.

Add three extra pages for Rs 8,000. You can also add a booking system (Rs 10,000) or a small ecommerce module up to 10 products (Rs 25,000).

Add-ons are listed on the Packages page and confirmed in your scope before work begins.

We guide you through a content brief and edit what you provide so it’s clear and consistent. Full copywriting from scratch isn’t included in launch packages — you know your business best.

For ecommerce, product description writing is available as an add-on at Rs 5,000 per 25 products.

03 / Care Plan & Support

How your site stays online after launch.

The Care Plan keeps your hosting, domain, email, backups, and security looked after — so launch day isn’t the day things start to slip.

Yes — for every launch client. At Rs 3,500/month, it covers the things a website needs to stay healthy: hosting, domain, email, daily backups, security, uptime monitoring, and minor updates.

We don’t hand off sites we can’t support. It’s how we keep the work we deliver in good standing.

Small text edits, swapping an image, updating opening hours, adding a team member, fixing a broken link. Anything that takes us under 30 minutes.

Larger changes — new pages, new sections, redesigns — are quoted separately or rolled into a site refresh.

You can pause or end the Care Plan at any time with one month’s notice. If you stop, we transfer the site files to you and you take over hosting, security, and updates yourself.

Most clients stay on the plan because the cost of one missed update or one expired domain is higher than a year of cover.

Direct WhatsApp support, included with Full and Premium Launch:

  • Full Launch — 60 days of WhatsApp support
  • Premium Launch — 90 days of WhatsApp support plus a 30-day post-launch check-in

Beyond that window, the Care Plan covers ongoing technical questions. Strategy and growth support continue through Social Continuation or Ads Management if you choose to extend.
