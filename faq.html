01 — Pricing & Payment

Deposits, installments, and what the price covers.

All pricing is published. Nothing is negotiated.

How do deposits and installments work?

Every launch package starts with a deposit, then balance in installments tied to delivery milestones.

  • Starter Launch — Rs 10,000 deposit, Rs 15,000 at handover.
  • Full Launch — Rs 26,000 deposit, then 2 installments of Rs 19,500.
  • Premium Launch — Rs 38,000 deposit, then 2 installments of Rs 28,500.
  • Ecommerce packages — 40% deposit, balance over 2 installments.
Is the pricing negotiable?

No. Prices are fixed and published so you know exactly what to expect. If a package doesn't fit, we'll point you to one that does, or to an add-on that closes the gap.

What if my needs sit between two packages?

Defined add-ons close the gap. You can add 3 pages (Rs 8,000), a booking system (Rs 10,000), a small ecommerce module up to 10 products (Rs 25,000), or a site refresh after 12 months (Rs 30,000).

Are there hidden costs after launch?

No. The Care Plan at Rs 3,500/month covers hosting, domain, email, backups, security, monitoring, and minor updates. Anything beyond that — ads, social continuation, larger updates — is a separate, published service.

02 — Scope & Delivery

Timelines, revisions, and what's included.

Each package has a defined scope. That keeps delivery predictable.

How long does each launch take?
  • Starter Launch — 30 days
  • Full Launch — 60 days
  • Premium Launch — 75 days
  • Ecommerce Starter — 45 days
  • Ecommerce Full — 60 days
  • Ecommerce Premium — 90 days

Timelines start once the scope is signed and the deposit is received.

How many revisions are included?

Each package includes defined revision rounds at fixed milestones — branding, design, and pre-launch. Orbitra is not for clients seeking unlimited revisions. That structure is what keeps the price and timeline firm.

What platforms do you build on?

Starter Launch is hosted on Vercel. Full and Premium Launch use WordPress on Teal Host. Ecommerce packages are built on Shopify. The platform is chosen to match the package, not the other way around.

What do you need from me to start?

A clear idea of your business, your offer, and your audience. The Launch Audit walks you through the rest. After the audit, you'll get a recommendation and scope document within 48 hours.

03 — Care Plan & Ongoing

What happens after launch.

The Care Plan is mandatory. It's how the site stays online, fast, and secure.

Is the Care Plan really mandatory?

Yes. The Care Plan (Rs 3,500/month) is required for every launch client. It covers hosting, domain, email, backups, security, monitoring, and minor updates. Without it, the site has no maintained foundation — and we won't launch a site we can't keep stable.

What counts as a "minor update" under the Care Plan?

Small text edits, image swaps, contact detail updates, opening hours, simple page tweaks. Larger work — new pages, new features, redesigns — is scoped separately as an add-on.

Do you manage ads or social media after launch?

Yes — as defined monthly services, available to completed launch clients.

  • Ads Management — Light Rs 10K, Standard Rs 15K, Active Rs 20K (plus minimum ad spend).
  • Social Continuation — Light Rs 12K, Standard Rs 18K, Active Rs 25K, extending the 1-month setup from Full or Premium Launch.
What if I want to leave the Care Plan later?

You can cancel. At that point the site, domain, and hosting move to your own accounts, and ongoing maintenance becomes your responsibility. We'll walk you through the handover step by step.
