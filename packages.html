Mandatory upkeep, so your site stays live.

A live site needs hosting, security, backups, and small fixes. The Care Plan handles all of that for every Orbitra client.

Care Plan
Rs 3,500Per month · required for all launches

Why it's required

Sites that aren't maintained break, get flagged by search engines, or lose data. The Care Plan keeps everything we built running, so you don't end up with a broken store six months in.

It also gives you a direct line for small content tweaks — new opening hours, a swapped image, an updated price — without needing to learn the back end.
