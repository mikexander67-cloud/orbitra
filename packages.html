Care Plan

Keep your site running, on your terms.

An optional plan that keeps hosting, domain, email, backups, and minor updates handled. Available in four commitment tiers — longer commitments unlock real savings.

TierPriceEffective
MonthlyRs 3,500 / monthno commitment
QuarterlyRs 9,500 / quarter≈ Rs 3,167/mo · ~10% off
Semi-annualRs 17,500 / 6 months≈ Rs 2,917/mo · ~17% off
AnnualRs 32,000 / year≈ Rs 2,667/mo · ~24% off
First-year bundle. Take the annual Care Plan at the same time as any launch package for Rs 28,000 — Rs 4,000 off the standard annual price. One-time, at point of launch. After the first year it renews at standard tier pricing.

What's included, every tier

  • Hosting, domain, and business email kept active
  • Daily backups and security monitoring
  • Up to 2 minor content updates per month
  • Further updates at Rs 1,500 each, billed transparently
  • A monthly performance snapshot

If you'd rather self-manage, that's fine too. Every package hands over full admin access and a usage guide. One-off support sessions are available at Rs 1,500/hour (30-minute minimum) if you need help later.
