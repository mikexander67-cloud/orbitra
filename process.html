The Build Journey

What happens between deposit and launch.

A typical Full Launch unfolds across four working stages. Starter is faster, Premium is longer — but the shape is the same.

Day 1 – 7

Discovery & brand

Brand questionnaire, content checklist, and the first visual direction. You confirm tone, colours, and voice.

Day 8 – 25

Design & copy

Page designs delivered for review. Copy drafted with you, not at you. One round of structured revisions per page.

Day 26 – 45

Build & integrate

The site is built on WordPress or Shopify. Social setup, email, and tracking are configured behind the scenes.

Day 46 – 60

Train & launch

Training calls, your business manual, and a soft launch window. You go live ready, not anxious.

Scope clarity

What is included — and what is not.

Published scope removes the awkward conversations later. Here is the line we draw on every launch package.

Included in every launch

  • A fixed price, paid in clear installments
  • A written scope document signed before work begins
  • Defined number of pages, revisions, and training calls
  • Hosting, domain, and email setup handled for you
  • Training so you can update content yourself
  • A business manual covering your tools and logins
  • WhatsApp support after handover
  • A defined launch date, agreed upfront

Not part of a launch package

  • Unlimited revisions or open-ended design exploration
  • Highly experimental or undefined business briefs
  • Ongoing content writing beyond the launch scope
  • Paid ad spend (handled separately if you add ads management)
  • Photography or product description writing (available as add-ons)
  • Custom development outside published add-ons
  • Work without a signed scope and deposit
  • Negotiated pricing — published rates apply to everyone

Anything outside the launch scope can usually be added — through a published add-on, a recurring service, or a separate engagement after launch. Care Plan is mandatory for all launch clients and begins the day your site goes live.
