About Orbitra

We build the digital foundation small businesses need to launch.

Orbitra is a Mauritian studio that turns websites, branding, and social presence into structured packages — with fixed scope, fixed prices, and step-by-step delivery. No mystery quotes. No endless revisions. Just a clear path from idea to live.

01 — Who we are

Built for the founder who wants to start right, not start over.

Most small businesses in Mauritius don't need an agency pitch. They need a website that works, a brand that looks consistent, and a social presence that's ready on day one. They need someone who explains things plainly and delivers what was promised.

That's what we do. We package the essentials into three launch tiers — Starter, Full, and Premium — and we walk you through every step. You always know what's included, what it costs, and when it ships.

Audience fit

Who Orbitra is for — and who it isn't.

We work best with founders who want structure. If that's you, we'll be a great fit. If not, we'll say so honestly.

A good fit

Built for you if…

  • You're launching or formalising a Mauritian business and need a digital presence ready.
  • You want fixed pricing and a clear timeline — not an open-ended project.
  • You're cash-conscious but willing to invest when the scope is defined.
  • You want to be guided, taught, and supported through the process.
  • You want to own and understand what you're launching.
Not the right fit

Probably not for you if…

  • You need a highly experimental or undefined creative brief.
  • Your business idea hasn't been decided yet.
  • You can't commit to a deposit upfront.
  • You expect unlimited revisions or scope changes mid-build.
  • You're looking for the cheapest possible vendor, not a structured partner.
Principles

Why we package instead of quote.

Custom projects are slow, opaque, and expensive. Packages are faster, fairer, and easier to commit to. These are the principles behind the model.

01

Fixed scope, fixed price

Every package lists exactly what's included. You see the price before you commit, and it doesn't change unless you ask for an add-on.

02

Timelines that hold

30, 60, 75 days — depending on the tier. Milestones are agreed up front. You always know the next step and when it lands.

03

Transparent payments

Deposit upfront, balance in clear installments. Every amount is published. Nothing is negotiated behind closed doors.

04

Education, not handover

Every launch includes training calls and a custom business manual. You leave knowing how to use what we built.

05

Support after launch

Every launch client moves onto a Care Plan. Hosting, backups, security, and minor updates stay handled — so the foundation doesn't drift.

06

Honest fit checks

If your project doesn't match our packages, we say so. We'd rather refer you elsewhere than oversell a wrong fit.
