02 · How we work

Six principles that shape every launch.

Orbitra is structured on purpose. The way we price, scope, and deliver is the product. These are the rules we hold to, on every package, every time.

P/01

Packages, not projects.

Fixed scope. Fixed price. Fixed timeline. You know what you are buying before you sign.

P/02

Published pricing.

Every package and add-on is listed on the site. No negotiation, no surprise quotes.

P/03

Step-by-step delivery.

Milestones, not vibes. You always know which phase you are in and what comes next.

P/04

Education included.

Training calls, manuals, and walkthroughs. You leave the launch able to run the site.

P/05

Care Plan is essential.

Every launch includes ongoing care. Hosting, backups, security, updates — supported monthly.

P/06

Calm, supportive contact.

WhatsApp support during build. Clear answers. No jargon. No pressure tactics.

03 · The right fit

Who Orbitra is for — and who it isn't.

Structured packages work brilliantly for some businesses and not at all for others. Read both columns before you book the audit.

A good fit if you are…

  • An early-stage or growing Mauritian business ready to formalise your digital presence.
  • Cash-aware, but willing to invest properly when the path is clear.
  • Open to learning how your website and brand work, with support.
  • Comfortable with a deposit, fixed scope, and milestone-based delivery.
  • Selling a defined service or a clear range of products.

Not the right fit if you need…

  • Highly experimental creative briefs with no defined deliverable.
  • An undefined business idea still searching for its model.
  • To start without paying a deposit upfront.
  • Unlimited revisions or open-ended scope without a fixed end.
  • A vendor who simply takes instructions without recommending a path.
04 · Tone & approach

The way we speak is part of the work.

Clear, calm, direct, supportive. We write to a smart owner who is new to digital, not a marketing specialist. That changes the words on the page.

↳ Words we use

launch set up ready structured package included step by step clear supported guided foundation start grow

Short sentences. Active voice. Helpful, not hyped.

↳ Words we avoid

bespoke stunning world-class dream journey transform elevate unleash magic infrastructure governance

No jargon. No marketing inflation. No promises we won't write into a scope.
