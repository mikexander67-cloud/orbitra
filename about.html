About Orbitra

A structured way to launch.

Orbitra builds the digital foundations small businesses need to launch — website, branding, and social presence — delivered as defined packages with installments, not custom projects.

The model

What Orbitra is — and what it isn't.

Orbitra is a structured launch agency for early-stage and growing Mauritian businesses. It sits between freelancers — cheap, but ad-hoc and hard to pin down — and full agencies, which are expensive and often unreachable for a small business. What Orbitra sells is launch readiness: the digital foundation a business needs to open online, plus the literacy to operate it once it's handed over.

It is not a freelance service provider. It is not a generic creative agency. It is not a high-end consultancy. And it does not take on custom one-off projects outside the defined packages. The packages are the product — that is what keeps the pricing fixed, the timelines clear, and the experience the same for every business that comes through.

Why packages, not custom quotes

A first-time buyer launching a business online is usually cautious and value clarity over endless options. They want to know exactly what they're buying, what it costs, what comes next, and how to use it. Open-ended quotes make that hard. A defined package with installments makes it simple — you can see the whole thing before you commit, and pay for it over two to three months rather than all at once.

Who we work with

A good fit, and an honest not-a-fit.

Orbitra works best for some businesses and genuinely isn't right for others. Here's the honest version.

A good fit

Early-stage and growing Mauritian businesses.

Service operators, small product brands, owner-operators — often launching their first real digital presence. Cash-constrained but willing to invest if the cost and scope are clear. People who want handholding and a bit of education, not just files dropped in a folder.

Not a fit

Where Orbitra isn't the right call.

Highly experimental creative briefs, or undefined business ideas still being figured out. Businesses that can't pay a deposit upfront. Anyone looking for unlimited revisions or fully custom creative direction — the packages are fixed, and that's the point. If that's what you need, a full agency will serve you better.

The approach

Built for where you're starting from.

Most small business owners we work with are smart, capable people who simply haven't had to set up a website, a brand, and a social presence before. They don't need jargon. They need a clear path, a fixed price, and someone to show them how it all works once it's theirs.

So every package ends the same way: with a handover. You get full admin access, your hosting and domain in your own name, a usage guide, and recordings of the training calls. From there you can keep it running yourself, or take the optional Care Plan and let Orbitra handle the upkeep. Either way, the business is yours — Orbitra just gets it launched, properly.
